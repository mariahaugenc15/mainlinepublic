The gap

The trades have been left behind

AI diagnostic tools are standard in healthcare, aviation, and automotive — industries where a wrong call costs lives or billions. Plumbing, HVAC, and electrical have the same stakes and the same complexity, but almost none of the tooling.

Meanwhile, a generation of master technicians is retiring, taking decades of diagnostic judgment with them. The industry needs a way to encode that knowledge and put it in the hands of the next generation — in the field, in real time.

What HauGen does

Not a chatbot. A diagnostic partner.

🧠

Branching diagnostic intelligence

A tech describes or photographs a symptom and HauGen walks them through a structured diagnostic path — guided reasoning, not a generic chatbot guess. Every branch is grounded in real defect taxonomies, not natural-language improvisation.

✅

Confidence-scored second opinions

When the AI isn't certain — or the issue is safety-critical — the case routes to a credentialed expert review layer before a part gets ordered or a repair gets made. The tech gets a real answer, not a disclaimer.

📊

Built on real trade data

Trained on defect taxonomies, inspection standards, and manufacturer technical bulletins used across the industry — and continuously improving from every job run through the platform. The system gets smarter with every diagnosis.

🔧

Works inside your existing tools

HauGen layers over the field service platforms companies already use. No rip-and-replace, no retraining, no new app for techs to adopt. It shows up where the work already happens.

Why HauGen, why now

The structural advantages compound over time

01
An overlooked market with real urgency

The skilled trades represent a $500B+ annual services market in North America. AI investment in this sector is a fraction of what healthcare, legal, and finance have seen — despite comparable diagnostic complexity and a worsening labor shortage that creates immediate demand for a solution that works in the field today.

02
Purpose-built reasoning, not a chatbot retrofit

HauGen is built from the ground up for diagnostic reasoning in a trade context — structured branching logic, calibrated confidence scoring, and safety escalation pathways. This is clinical decision support for plumbing, not a scheduling tool with a chat window bolted on. That distinction is the product.

03
A data and network advantage that's hard to replicate

Every job run through HauGen improves the model. Every credentialed reviewer added to the network deepens the second-opinion layer. These are structural advantages that accrue over time — a competitor starting today faces a compounding gap, not just a feature gap.

04
A B2B SaaS model built for the way trade businesses operate

HauGen sells to the company, not the technician. Each company runs on its own data, its own customer history, its own pricing. That's not a limitation — it's the right architecture for a trade business's operating reality. HauGen is the platform they run on, branded as their own.
